### Risks / possible imbalance

- Metropole still slightly above other profiles in portal rate; keep watching if it becomes too safe.
- Celeiro is execution-sensitive: missing one key piece (Flow/Navigator/Intendente) often drops below 1500.
- Spawn distance still matters: without Navigator, far spawns can fail by ETA even with decent economy.

### Practical tuning ideas

- Keep Bastiao non-passive: strong wall + good execution already protects villages without free immunity.
- Add a small D90 consistency buff for Celeiro (not D120) to reduce 50-100 point misses.
- Keep ETA risk for players who skip Navigator, but reduce late-start variance for otherwise good runs.
